VISION

“In a world were everything is segregated by gender, we believe that everyone should choose the things they feel like doing and buying without having the underlying pressure of trying to fit in a box.”

VALUES

Gender neutral

A brand that does not conform to gender norms. We do not segregate, judge, or market towards gender norms. Everyone should be comfortable with our brand.

Expressivenss

Feel free to express yourself and launch what you feel. Nobody should tell you how to act, smell or how to dress yourself.

Creativity

Unleash everything you have inside. Be creative with your choices in any way you could imagine. Mix things up, try new things and don’t be afraid to show how amazing you actually are!

Inclusivness

Nobody should be judged on who they love, what they look like or who they are. Inclusive marketing, of all genders, cultures, sexualities and lifestyles or of the utmost importance to our brand.
